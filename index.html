Asked before every first rental

Do I get charged when I send a request?

No. Requests are free — you aren’t charged until the owner accepts. Curators see who’s asking before anything moves, and you can sort out timing in chat first.

What if I break something?

Tell your curator — accidents are part of gear that actually gets used. Pickup and return photos document the condition on both ends, so there’s no he-said-she-said, and deposits and protection policies cover what the photos show. Details are in each listing before you request.

How do I know the gear isn’t junk?

Every listing shows the actual owner, everything that’s included, and original condition photos — no stock images. This is gear that’s loved and used, listed by the person who uses it.

How does pickup actually work?

You agree a time in chat and meet your curator — most handoffs take five minutes. Photos at pickup and return document the rental, and you get the one tip only the owner knows.

What does Cache take if I list my gear?

You keep 90% of every rental; Cache takes 10%. No listing fees, no hidden cuts — you see the split before you post. You approve every request, and two weekend bookings a month is about $162.

Where is Cache live?

San Diego, right now — Ocean Beach to North Park. Salt Lake City, Seattle, and San Francisco are next. Listings show the neighborhood so you know how far the gear is before you request.
